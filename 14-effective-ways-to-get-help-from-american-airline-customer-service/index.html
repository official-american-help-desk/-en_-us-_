How Do I Speak to a Live Agent at American Airlines?

  1. Sign in to your American Airlines account.
  2. Navigate to the Help Center.
  3. Click on “Contact Us” and select the trip you need help with.
  4. Choose the topic that best matches your issue (such as cancellations, refunds, or booking problems).
  5. Scroll down and select either “Call Us” or “Chat with an agent.”
